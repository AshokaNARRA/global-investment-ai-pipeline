# Global Investment & Market Intelligence Pipeline

An end-to-end investment intelligence ecosystem utilizing Agentic AI to automate global macro research, quantitatively screen equities for high-alpha opportunities, and deliver cross-border portfolio analytics via a unified BI dashboard.

---

## 🚀 System Architecture & Core Modules

### 1. US Market Sentiment Scraper (Agent 1)
* **Objective:** Automate the extraction and synthesis of unstructured global macro data.
* **Technology:** Python, Google Antigravity (Agentic AI framework), NLP libraries.
* **Functionality:** Scrapes real-time US financial news, press releases, and economic indicators. Uses NLP to score sentiment and flag significant market shifts, reducing manual research time by 80%.

### 2. US Equity Alpha Screener (Agent 2)
* **Objective:** Automate systematic "deal sourcing" and alpha identification.
* **Technology:** Python, Yahoo Finance API / Alpha Vantage, Pandas.
* **Functionality:** Runs quantitative screening models on US stock data to automatically filter, rank, and shortlist companies showing strong multi-bagger potential and high-alpha characteristics.

### 3. Indian Market Growth Screener (Agent 3)
* **Objective:** Cross-border equity analysis tailored to high-growth emerging markets.
* **Technology:** Python, NSE/BSE data feeds, Data Modeling algorithms.
* **Functionality:** Implements systematic filtering on the Indian public equity markets to cleanly isolate large-cap sector leaders and high-momentum growth assets.

### 4. Unified Investment Intelligence Dashboard
* **Objective:** Centralize multi-currency operations and risk tracking into a single source of truth.
* **Technology:** Tableau / Advanced Excel, Multi-currency API feeds (USD/INR/EUR).
* **Functionality:** Consolidates data from live trading accounts (e.g., eToro, Zerodha) and links seamlessly with the scraping and screening agents. Dynamically tracks core portfolio performance attribution and risk metrics including Alpha, Beta, and Sharpe Ratio.
